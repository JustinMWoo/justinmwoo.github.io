Overview

This expansion for the Marion Surgical Robot Simulator uses many of the tools from Minimally Invasive in a context that is more similar to that of a traditional laparoscopic surgery in order to provide a more interesting way for surgeons and surgeons in training to practice their surgical skils. In the expansion, the player performs surgery on aliens from across the galaxy in a series of novel procedures.

Gameplay Overview

The DLC contains twelve brand new surgical exercises that use five tools reimagined from those in Minimally Invasive. These levels include tasks such as injection of fluids using a syringe, balancing organs in the body, cutting tissues, burning infections and more! The player is also provided with an descriptive medical history and patient information to provide an immersive environment.
